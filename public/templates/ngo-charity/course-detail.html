Course Description

This comprehensive Digital Literacy Fundamentals course is designed to equip learners with essential computer skills and internet literacy needed to succeed in today's digital world. Whether you're a complete beginner or looking to strengthen your digital foundation, this course provides practical, hands-on learning experiences.

What You'll Learn

Basic computer operations and file management
Internet browsing and online safety practices
Email communication and digital etiquette
Microsoft Office basics (Word, Excel, PowerPoint)
Online collaboration tools and cloud storage
Digital citizenship and privacy protection

Prerequisites

No prior experience required. This course is designed for complete beginners who want to build a strong foundation in digital literacy.
