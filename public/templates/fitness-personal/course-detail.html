Yoga for Beginners

This course is designed for individuals new to yoga, focusing on foundational poses, breathing techniques, and mindfulness practices. Participants will learn to build strength, flexibility, and balance while reducing stress and improving overall well-being.

Meet Your Instructor

Course Curriculum

Week 1: Introduction to Yoga

This week covers the basics of yoga, including proper alignment, breathing techniques, and an introduction to common poses.

Week 2: Foundational Poses

Dive deeper into fundamental yoga poses, focusing on building strength, stability, and body awareness. We'll break down each pose to ensure safe and effective practice.

Week 3: Breathing and Mindfulness

Explore the power of pranayama (breathing techniques) and meditation to calm the mind, reduce stress, and cultivate a deeper connection to the present moment.
